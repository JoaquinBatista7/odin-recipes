Pizza

Description

The classic pizza with mozzarella, an exquisite dish which is very simple to make and which has spread from Italy to the whole world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Tomato sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°C (482°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  5. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and add fresh basil leaves.
  8. Season with a pinch of salt and serve immediately.
