Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

paghetti Aglio e Olio is a simple yet flavorful Italian pasta dish made with just a few ingredients: garlic, olive oil, red pepper flakes, and parsley. It’s a quick, easy meal that brings out the natural flavors of the ingredients, making it perfect for a light yet satisfying dinner."

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic (thinly sliced)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat extra virgin olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced garlic and sauté until golden brown. Be careful not to burn it.
  4. Add red pepper flakes to the pan and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the garlic and oil.
  6. Toss the pasta in the oil until well-coated. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and mix in fresh parsley.
  8. Serve with Parmesan cheese if desired.
