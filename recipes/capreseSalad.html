Caprese Salad

Description

The classic salad for any meal

Ingredients

  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced)
  • Tomatoes (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes into even rounds.
  2. Arrange the mozzarella and tomato slices alternately on a serving plate.
  3. Place fresh basil leaves between the slices of cheese and tomato.
  4. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad.
  5. Optionally, add a drizzle of balsamic vinegar for extra flavor.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator before serving.
